Gabs' Pasta

Gabs likes to make this pasta all the time since Covid

This is originally Gigi Hadid's pasta

but since Gabs started making it, it is now Gabs' pasta

Ingredients

  • Fresh Pasta
  • Tomato Paste
  • Heavy Cream
  • Brown Sugar
  • Red Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red Chilli Flakes

Steps

  1. Wash and prepare onions
  2. Boil water and add a pinch of salt to the water
  3. Cut onions and put garlic into the grinder
  4. Add pasta to water
  5. Throw onions and garlic into sauce pan to cook
  6. Add tomato paste to sauce pan and cook until brown
  7. Add heavy cream to sauce pan
  8. Bring to a simmer
  9. Season sauce with salt, pepper, and chilli flakes
  10. Drain pasta, save some pasta water for sauce
  11. Mix in pasta with sauce
  12. Enjoy!
